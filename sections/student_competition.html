2021 ArcGIS Online Competition for US HS+MS Students
A mapping competition open to high school and middle school students in the United States

Esri's 2021 ArcGIS US School Competition is open to high school (gr.9-12) and middle school (gr.4-8) students in the US who can analyze, interpret, and present data via an ArcGIS storymap or web app.
