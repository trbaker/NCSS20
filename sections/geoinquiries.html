GeoInquiries

GeoInquiries™ are free, standards-based inquiry activities for teaching map-based content found in commonly used textbooks. Each activity is designed using a common inquiry model. Collections of 15-20 activities per topic enhance your curriculum throughout the year.
 

Social studies collections include:
  1. Physical geography/Earth Science

  2. US History

  3. World History

  4. Government/Civics

  5. World Geography

  6. Human Geography
